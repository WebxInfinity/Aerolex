Quality

Aerolex Cables are manufactured to meet most world standard requirements :



Quality Policy :

Commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has always been a priority to Aerolex Cables. International norms & standards like IEC, CENELEC, UL, CSA, IS, BS, AS, NZS, VDE, AFNOR are strictly applied to all our cable designs. Our manufacturing facility is ISO 9001:2015 certified to guarantee premium quality for our products.

Our laboratory is well equipped to perform rigorous testing in accordance with international standards. The testing laboratory has all the necessary equipments for mechanical and electrical testing of raw materials, in-process goods and finished goods.

Through continuous improvement, Aerolex Cable strives to provide premium quality to ensure success and satisfaction of our customers. To achieve the highest quality standards, all Aerolex Cable products are manufactured with only premium quality copper, aluminium, PVC & rubber compounds and materials to our exacting specifications.
