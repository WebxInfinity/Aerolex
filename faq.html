FAQs for Submersible Pump Cables



What Is AWG ?

AWG stands for American Wire Gauge.

What are Submersible Pump Cables ?

The Submersible Pump Cables are the Cables used in well casing for supplying current to the Submersible Pump. Once the cable is enclosed it is not subjected to repetitive handling during rapid servicing of the entire Submersible Pump Unit.

What types of cables are available in Submersible Pump Cables Category?

The Submersible Pump Cables are mainly available in Flat and Round types of Cables. Both the Round and Flat cables are categorized as 3 cores (wire) - twisted together and 4 cores (wire) cables.

What is the maximum voltage for the conductor used in Submersible Pump Cables?

The maximum voltage used for Submersible Pump Cables is 600 Volts.

What is the maximum and minimum operating temperature for the conductor used in Submersible Pump Cables?

The maximum operating temperature of the conductor is 75 0 C and the minimum operating temperature of the Submersible Pump Cables is 25 0 C .

What does this 12 Strd 2 w/GRD Mean?

This means that the Pump Cable has 12 AWG in a single core (wire) conductor and contains 3 such cores (wire) including 1 ground core (wire)

What is the type of the conductor been used in Submersible Pump Cables?

The conductors used in Submersible Pump Cables are Solid Conductors which are made up of soft and uncoated Copper per ASTM B-3 (Standard Specification for Soft or Annealed Copper Wire)

What type of insulation is used in Submersible Pump Cables?

The insulation been used in the submersible pump cables is Color Coded PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)

What is the Color standard specification (US Standards) of 3 Wire (including ground) Submersible Pump Cables

The color specification of 3 wire Submersible Pump Cables including ground is Black and Red Cores with Green as a third Ground Core.

What is the Color standard specification (US Standards) of 4 Wire (including ground) Submersible Pump Cables

The color specification of 4 wire Submersible Pump Cables including ground is Black, Yellow and Red Cores with Green as a fourth Ground Core.
